Building Rhythms w/ Konnakkol
With Sonikblasts · Thu 28 May · 6:30pm · Secret Location near Jannowitzbrücke
Introduces Konnakkol, the South Indian classical rhythmic language, as a practical and creative way to understand rhythm. We'll build simple rhythmic patterns and layer them to explore polymeter and polyrhythms through listening, voice, and playful experimentation. Everyone welcome, no experience required.
Sign Up ↗
Building a Soundsystem - Take-aways
With Femme Bass Mafia (Lilia & Jenny) · Thu 28 May · 6:30pm · Secret Location near Jannowitzbrücke
Ever wondered what it takes to build a soundsystem? Femme Bass Mafia share the lessons, challenges, and insights from building their own "Angel Audio".
Sign Up ↗
Feed & Feedback
Hosted by O.M.Theorem & 16 Faces · Fri 29 May · 6:30pm · MaHalla, Berlin
A production feedback session centred around real-time listening and intimate community building over food. On Femme Bass Mafia's Angel Audio Soundsystem. Submit your track by 26 May 10am, or sign up to come listen.
Sign Up ↗
KüFa - Community Kitchen
With Torhaus Koch Kollective · Fri 29 May · 5:30pm · MaHalla, Berlin
Whether you're a seasoned chef, a food-waste warrior, or just looking for good food and good company, the KüFa is for you. Free entry, everyone welcome, signup mandatory.
Sign Up ↗
The Soundsystem MC Lab
With Roots Daughters & Trainah · Sat 30 May · 5:30pm · MaHalla, Berlin
Word, Sound and Power. Learn about soundsystem culture, key MC phrases & verses, hosting a session and using a pre-amp while MCing — on Roots Daughters Soundsystem. Ends with a cypher jam.
Sign Up ↗
A Dub&Dal original
Soundmash
Warrior Queen MC Yallah Roots Daughters Angel Audio
A reinterpretation of the traditional Jamaican Soundclash into a collaborative, women-led celebration of sonic exchange. Featuring Germany's only female-operated soundsystems,each crew showcasing music that sounds best on their handbuilt systems. Warrior Queen from Jamaica and MC Yallah take SOUNDMASH to another level entirely.

Three years ago, it was one room, one soundsystem, one pot of dal. Like a good dal, it's been slowly simmering, thanks to you all, we're ready to feed you for three days with heartwarming food, two handbuilt soundsystems and diverse South Asian culture.

Dub & Dal is about celebrating roots and community, creating spaces around the world that feel like home, but are accessible to everyone.
